Professor S Vengadesan

Dr. S Vengadesan currently serves as a Professor in the Department of Applied Mechanics and Biomedical Engineering at IIT Madras and heads the Computational Fluid Dynamics Lab. So far he has supervised 8 PhD and 25 MS scholars. He has carried out 17 sponsored and industrial consultancy projects. He has published 85 peer reviewed publications. He received his PhD from Kobe University in Japan. His research interests include multiphase flow, heat transfer augmentation, turbulence modeling, LES/DES, and unsteady aerodynamics.
