Professor S Vengadesan

Dr. S Vengadesan currently serves as a Professor in the Department of Applied Mechanics at IIT Madras. He also heads the Computational Fluid Dynamics Lab which is a part of the Fluid Mechanics group. He received his Ph.D from Kobe University in Japan. His research interests include multiphase flow, heat transfer augmentation, turbulence modeling, LES/DES, turbulence modeling, and unsteady aerodynamics.
